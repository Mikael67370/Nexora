Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13 February 2026

1. Who We Are

Nexora Ltd ("Nexora", "we", "us") provides professional turnover cleaning and property operations services for short-let hosts in London. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our website or services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you interact with our website or book a service:

  • Contact details: name, email address, phone number, WhatsApp number
  • Property details: address, property type, access instructions
  • Payment information: processed securely via Stripe — we do not store card details
  • Communications: messages sent via WhatsApp or other channels
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, pages visited (via cookies)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Process and fulfil your bookings
  • Communicate with you about your service (confirmations, updates, photo reports)
  • Process payments securely through Stripe
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Payment Security

All payments are processed through Stripe, a PCI DSS Level 1 certified payment processor. We never have access to your full card details. Stripe's privacy policy can be found at stripe.com/privacy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Stripe: for payment processing
  • Our cleaning team: property address and access details only, as needed to deliver the service
  • Legal authorities: if required by law

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. Booking records are kept for up to 6 years for accounting purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

7. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Data portability

8. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies for essential functionality. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

9. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or requests, contact us via WhatsApp at +33 7 69 81 23 91.
