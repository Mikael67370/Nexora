Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 13 February 2026

1. Cancellation by the Client

We understand plans can change. Our cancellation terms are based on how much notice you provide before the scheduled service:

Notice Period Refund
More than 48 hours before service Full deposit refund
24 – 48 hours before service 50% deposit refund
Less than 24 hours before service No refund

All cancellations must be communicated via WhatsApp at +33 7 69 81 23 91. The cancellation time is based on when the message is received and acknowledged.

2. Rescheduling

You may reschedule your booking free of charge if you notify us at least 24 hours before the original service time. Rescheduling requests made with less than 24 hours' notice will be treated as a cancellation under the terms above.

Rescheduled services are subject to availability.

3. No-Show / No Access

If our team arrives at the property and is unable to gain access (e.g. wrong code, locked key safe, no response), we will attempt to contact you via WhatsApp. If access is not provided within 30 minutes, the visit will be treated as a completed booking and no refund will be issued.

4. Cancellation by Nexora

In rare circumstances, we may need to cancel or reschedule a booking (e.g. severe weather, team emergency). In such cases:

  • We will notify you as soon as possible via WhatsApp
  • We will offer a priority rescheduled slot at no extra cost
  • If rescheduling is not possible, a full refund will be issued

5. Refund Processing

All refunds are processed through Stripe and returned to the original payment method. Refunds typically appear within 5–10 business days, depending on your bank.

6. Disputes

If you believe a cancellation charge has been applied unfairly, please contact us via WhatsApp. We review all disputes on a case-by-case basis and aim to resolve them within 48 hours.

7. Contact

For cancellations, rescheduling, or questions about this policy, reach us via WhatsApp at +33 7 69 81 23 91.
